# Welcome to ForestNotes 🌳

ForestNotes is a local-first Markdown note-taking app that turns your tags into a living Knowledge Forest.

## Core Concepts

- **Markdown files** are the single source of truth — all your notes stay on disk as `.md` files
- **Tags** grow into trees in the Knowledge Forest
- **Forest View** visualizes your knowledge structure using #getting-started D3.js force layout

## Quick Start

1. Open a local folder as your vault
2. Create or edit notes with tags
3. Switch to **Forest** view to see your knowledge landscape
4. Search across all notes with the search bar

## Tips

- Use `#tags` inline or in YAML frontmatter
- The more you read and update a topic, the healthier its tree
- Neglected topics slowly fade — a gentle reminder to review
- Tags that appear together grow connections (vines)

Happy note-taking!
